Steel Cut Oats


We call these pinhead oats in Ireland and Great Britain. These style of oats are heartier and chewier than there inferior rolled oats. Once I had pinhead oats with this recipe, I never went back to rolled oats. Serving: 4
Preperation time: 5 minutes
Cook time: 25 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of steel cut oats
  • 1 1/2 cups of almond milk
  • 1 1/2 cups of water
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt

Instructions

  1. Bring 1 1/2 cups water and 1 1/2 cups of almond milk with 1/4 teaspoon salt to a boil then add one cup steel cut oats with 1/2 teaspoon of cinnamon.
  2. Reduce heat to a low simmer. Cook for 20 - 25 minutes and stir occasionally. Add more liquid if needed!
  3. Remove from heat and let stand covered for a couple of minutes.
